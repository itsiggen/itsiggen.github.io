Hello there!


I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in Computer Science at KU Leuven. I work at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Computer Security, specializing in Adversarial Machine Learning and Reinforcement Learning.

My research focuses on both practical and theoretical aspects of adversarial attacks and defenses, across a broad range of systems and modalities. In web automation, I have broken Google reCaptcha v3 by leveraging its scoring mechanism as feedback to train agents that avoid detection. In malware detection, in collaboration with UCL, KCL, and Avast, I have successfully led the development of a novel approach to harden the AI module of Avast Antivirus against adversarial malware. In computer vision, I have created a general framework for reliably evaluating the robustness of any deployed AI model. By adapting and optimizing attacks and defenses under the competitive game they form, vulnerabilities are systematically exposed, allowing for their identification and removal.

More generally, I explore the fundamentals of robust learning and its adversarial and counterfactual facets as essential components in achieving trustworthy and safe AI. As human decision-making increasingly transitions to automated, I am developing new techniques and methodologies for training models resilient to evasion and other failure modes. Currently, I focus on safeguarding LLMs through adversarial training, alignment, and activation engineering, to ensure safe and correct outputs in real-world deployments.

However, my aspirations do not end with practical methods for robustness. Towards general and safe AI, my research endeavors to prevent reward hacking and specification gaming through constrained and structured learning. Intelligence is by its nature self-limiting, as it cannot pursue a reward unstrategically and without constraints. Yet intelligence is also transgressive, as it cannot perpetually linger in a known set of constraints.

Beyond academia, as a restless spirit I try to avoid hobbies, but I still have several interests: I am a competitive capoeirista and water polo player, and in my free time I enjoy science fiction, creative writing, and tabletop/computer games. For everything else, feel free to drop me a line!
